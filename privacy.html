This Privacy Policy explains how WearCast (“we”, “us”) processes personal data when you use this website. WearCast is a weather-based clothing recommendation app.

Controller / Contact

Controller: Moeez Aziz
Contact: moeezazizch@gmail.com

What data is processed

  • Location data (optional): If you choose “Use my location”, your browser may provide approximate coordinates. These coordinates are used to fetch local weather.
  • Search queries (optional): If you type a place name, that query is sent to a geocoding provider to resolve coordinates.
  • Functional storage (optional): If enabled, WearCast stores your preferences and last-used location in your browser storage.
  • Usage analytics (always on): WearCast uses GoatCounter to count page views and basic usage metrics. GoatCounter is designed to be privacy-friendly and does not use cookies or localStorage for tracking page views.

Purposes & legal bases (GDPR)

  • Provide the service (weather lookup, recommendations): Art. 6(1)(b) (performance of a contract / providing a requested service) and/or Art. 6(1)(f) (legitimate interest in operating the website).
  • Device location (only if you choose it): Art. 6(1)(a) (consent). You can withdraw by disabling location permission in your browser/OS settings.
  • Functional storage (preferences/last location): Art. 6(1)(a) (consent). You can withdraw by disabling “Functional storage” in the Privacy choices dialog and clearing site data.
  • Analytics (GoatCounter, always on): we rely on Art. 6(1)(f) (legitimate interest) to understand general usage and improve the site. GoatCounter is used as privacy-friendly analytics (no advertising, no cross-site tracking, no cookies used for pageview tracking). See GoatCounter’s GDPR notes: goatcounter.com/help/gdpr.

Data recipients / processors

  • Open‑Meteo (weather API): open-meteo.com
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim (geocoding / reverse geocoding): nominatim.openstreetmap.org
  • GoatCounter (analytics): goatcounter.com
  • GitHub Pages (hosting): pages.github.com

Retention

  • Functional storage: kept in your browser until you disable it and/or clear site data.
  • Device location: processed on demand; WearCast does not store your precise coordinates unless functional storage is enabled and you allow it.
  • Analytics: analytics retention follows GoatCounter’s defaults and service retention practices.

Your rights (EEA/UK)

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access, rectification, deletion
  • Restriction and objection
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise rights, contact the controller at the address above.

Cookies

WearCast does not use advertising cookies. Functional browser storage may be used if you enable it. GoatCounter analytics is always enabled and is designed to be privacy-friendly.
