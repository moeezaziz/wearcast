WearCast helps you decide what to wear today with weather-smart outfit recommendations that improve as you add your own wardrobe.

### Short description

Get a weather-smart outfit recommendation for today, then make it better with the clothes you already own.

## Full Description

WearCast gives you a better answer to what to wear today.

Start with live weather for your location, then get a daily outfit recommendation that fits the temperature, rain risk, wind, and overall feel of the day.

Add a few staples from your own wardrobe and WearCast starts building recommendations from the clothes you actually own.

WearCast is made for people who want to get dressed faster, look more intentional, and stop second-guessing the weather.

Core features:

- weather-smart outfit recommendations
- comfort and style tuning
- wardrobe-powered daily picks
- photo-first wardrobe capture
- saved looks
- account sync across devices

## In-App Copy Alignment Notes

Use these phrases across launch-facing surfaces:

- what to wear today
- weather-smart outfit recommendation
- from your closet
- clothes you already own
- daily outfit picks

Avoid these phrases in launch materials:

- forecast-led styling
- unlock your wardrobe
- product validation
- profile tab validation
- AI fashion engine
