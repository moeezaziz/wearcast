# WearCast Beta Test Plan

## Goal

Run a small beta that validates activation, wardrobe setup, and willingness to pay before a broader public push.

## Recommended Cohort

- 30 to 100 iPhone users
- mix of frequent commuters, office workers, students, and style-aware casual users
- at least a few users who are willing to add 5 to 15 wardrobe items

## Beta Questions To Answer

- Do people reach first value in under a minute?
- Does the wardrobe setup feel worth the effort?
- Do recommendations feel trustworthy enough to revisit?
- Are the paywall triggers landing after value rather than too early?
- Are real purchase and restore flows working cleanly?

## Core Tasks For Testers

1. Install the app and get a first recommendation.
2. Try both location permission and manual city search.
3. Complete the quick-tune step.
4. Add at least 5 wardrobe items.
5. Try one photo scan and one manual add.
6. Save at least one look.
7. Trigger the paywall intentionally.
8. If eligible, test trial or subscription flow.

## Metrics To Watch During Beta

- first recommendation rate
- first item add rate
- 5-item completion rate
- scan success rate
- paywall view rate
- purchase start rate
- restore success rate
- crash volume

## Exit Criteria For Soft Launch

- onboarding feels stable across fresh installs
- no major auth or sync failures remain
- no major subscription blocker remains
- recommendation failures are rare and diagnosable
- top 5 onboarding or monetization dropoffs have known next actions
